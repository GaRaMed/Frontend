Privacy Policy

This privacy policy (hereinafter referred to as the "Policy") explains how the GaRaMed website (https://garamed.online/) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we process your information when you use our website.

Information Collection

We collect only the necessary information to ensure the functioning of the website and the provision of our services. This information includes:

  • Personal data: Name, email address, social media links, and other data you provide during registration or subscription.
  • Visit data: We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your behavior on the website, including visit times, pages viewed, and devices used to access the site.

Information Use

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our services.
  • Notifying you about new materials, updates, and news that may interest you.
  • Analyzing and improving the user experience on the website.
  • Support and answering your questions or requests.

Information Protection

We take measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, including data encryption and regular security checks.

Information Disclosure

We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • If required by law or in response to legal requests.
  • If necessary to protect our rights or fulfill our obligations to you.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience and to collect information about user behavior. You can configure your browser to refuse the use of cookies, but this may affect the functionality of the site.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data and its correction.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out of receiving notifications or marketing materials at any time.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this policy at any time. All changes will be notified on this page with the date of the last update indicated.

Contacts

If you have any questions or comments regarding this policy or the processing of your data, please contact us through one of our contact forms or via email: garamedinfo@gmail.com.
